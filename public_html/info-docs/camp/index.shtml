Spartans Youth Football Camps

This camp is a 3-day non-padded camp for 4th & 5th graders or 6th - 8th graders Monday July 24th thru Wednesday 26th. The focus of the camp is on skill development and team play. The Memorial Spartan Football staff along with the Varsity players help coach and assist the camp. Players that register by July 1, 2017 will receive a camp t-shirt. Cleats and workout attire are strongly recommended.
